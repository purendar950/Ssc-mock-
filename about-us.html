About ExamZen

ExamZen is an exam-preparation platform built for aspirants of Indian government recruitment exams — SSC (CGL, CHSL, MTS), Railway (NTPC, Group D), and various State exams.

Our goal is simple: make high-quality, exam-pattern mock tests affordable and accessible to every aspirant, in both English and हिन्दी.

What we offer

  • Full-length, sectional, and subject-wise mock tests modelled on the latest exam pattern.
  • Previous Year Question (PYQ) sets with detailed bilingual explanations.
  • Instant results with section-wise analysis and All-India ranking.
  • Free daily live tests and demo mocks for every exam.

Our promise

Honest pricing, no hidden subscriptions, and a relentless focus on quality content. One affordable annual plan unlocks everything.
