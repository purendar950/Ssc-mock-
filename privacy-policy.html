Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ExamZen ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our platform.

Information we collect

  • Account data: your name, username, and email address provided at registration.
  • Usage data: test attempts, scores, saved questions, and progress.
  • Payment data: for premium purchases we record the transaction reference (UTR), amount, and any coupon used. We do not store card or bank credentials.

How we use it

  • To provide and personalize your test-taking experience.
  • To compute results, analytics, and All-India ranking.
  • To verify and activate premium purchases.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be processed by infrastructure providers (e.g., hosting and database services) strictly to operate the platform.

Your choices

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting support@examzen.app.
