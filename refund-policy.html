Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026

ExamZen sells digital access to mock tests and study content. Please read this policy before purchasing.

Digital product

Because premium grants immediate access to digital content (mocks, PYQs, analytics), purchases are generally non-refundable once access is activated.

When refunds apply

  • Duplicate payment: if you were charged twice for the same plan, the extra amount is fully refunded.
  • Activation failure: if premium could not be activated and we are unable to resolve it within 7 days, you are eligible for a full refund.

How to request

Email support@examzen.app with your registered username and the payment UTR/reference number within 7 days of the transaction. Approved refunds are processed to the original UPI within 5–7 working days.
